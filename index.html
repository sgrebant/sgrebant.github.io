personAbout

I was a PhD student at University of Lille from 2020 to 2023. I am now a software engineer.

My recent researches focus on tree-based parametric worst-case execution time (WCET) analysis. In particular, these researches relate to the following topics:

  1. Representing infeasible paths in tree-based WCET analysis
  2. Modeling the effect of hardware components on the WCET
  3. Combination of abstract interpretation analyses with WCET analyses
